About me

I know many frontend engineers don't enjoy working with CSS, and I understand it can be challenging. However, my perspective is different. I started my career working solely with CSS and HTML. I've attended numerous CSS conferences, completed many courses, read books and tons of articles to build solid CSS skills. I truly enjoy working with it.

Since 2017, I have focused on ReactJS. I particularly enjoy its component-based architecture, which allows me to divide UI elements into smaller, reusable components. I've been impressed by its performance and scalability, making it an ideal choice for large-scale applications.

I am confident enough to admit my mistakes, learn from them, and fix them. I am not afraid to ask for help when I need it. I always keep an open mind when it comes to feedback and constructive criticism. I place great importance on open and transparent communication with my colleagues, fostering a healthy work environment.

Working remotely comes naturally to me; I have experience collaborating with teams across different time zones and locations. I'm comfortable working independently, but I also appreciate the benefits of working in an office, including the opportunity to connect with colleagues and build meaningful relationships.

Please feel free to ask any questions you may have, or if you'd like to discuss my potential contributions to your project.
